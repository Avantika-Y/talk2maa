ABOUT US

Being a mother is like being reborn.Having a whole ecosystem supporting you during your motherhood is true blissfulness.Talk2 maa solves all your doubts and concerns about new pregnancy,in clear and simple way.As we know that we are all products of our upbringing and socialization-our brains are the product of implicit and explicit conditioning.
Whether you’re just preparing to become parents, trying to conceive, already pregnant, have just stepped into the mom & dad club or are you are just worried about your baby’s growth & developmental milestones.
Talk2maa is your go-to site for all your queries!We believe that everyone deserves a positive journey from pregnant phase to birth to every year of parenting. With this pregnancy and baby app, Moms & dads should have access to the information of millions of parents who share the experience of parenthood.
